Gautam Buddha University

About

Gautam Buddha University ("GBU") (Hindi: गौतम बुद्ध विश्वविद्यालय) is a government university located in Greater Noida, Gautam Buddha Nagar district. Uttar Pradesh, India.It is one of Uttar Pradesh's state government university which commenced its first academic session in the year 2008.University campus is spread over 511 acres in Greater Noida(Delhi-NCR) and offers Bachelors,Masters and Doctoral degrees in Engineering,Business Administration,Computer Applications, Biotechnology and Buddhist Studies and is mainly focused on research..Gautam Buddha University has signed a Memorandum of Understanding(MoU) with National Skill Development Corporation for introducing skill development programmes in University and is one of three universities in India to do so.

Campus

Campus of Gautam Buddha University is situated in Greater Noida near Pari Chowk with a 511-acre campus.

Schools

School of Management

School of Management MBA was the first course started by GBU in 2008.The school has classrooms computing facilities supported by a library of books, journals, films and databases. It has specialisation groups (HRM, Marketing, Finance, Operations, and Strategy etc.) that organises weekly activities. GBU-SOM also has a 500-kW solar roof top which provides electricity to Greater Noida.

School of Biotechnology

The Department of Biotechnology is an interdisciplinary Department with an amalgamation of expertise from diverse areas of basic and applied biology, mathematics, microbial, plant, animal sciences and computer sciences.

School of Information and Communication Technology

Department of Computer Science, Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

School of Engineering

The Gautam Buddha University School of Engineering offers leadership opportunities with technical and professional augmentation establishments viz. the GBU-IEEE student chapter etc. Integrated master's degree programs are offered through the SOE. The college is enriched by Civil, Electrical, Mechanical Engineering and Architecture and Regional Planning departments.Each department has its own administration structure with HOD at top of it.Department of Civil Engineering and Architecture will run from School of Architecture and built environment from next year.

School of Law, Justice and Governance

The school fosters advancement of learning, teaching and research in of law, justice and governance. One of the main academic programmes, the five-year integrated BA LLB, was started in July 2012. At the time of admission in the first semester, the number of students enrolled in the course was 42. In the first semester of the programme, students participate in moot court practice, debate, essay writing and project works. The school fosters advancement of learning, teaching and research in of law.

School of Buddhist Studies and Civilization

The school offers academic programmes at post-graduate and research level including M.A., M.Phil., and Ph.D. It gives knowledge of Buddhist History, Philosophy, and Literature in Pali, Sanskrit, Chinese, Burmese, Sinhalese and Tibetan languages and their literature. With a very rich library, a meditation centre, and a serene campus, the school enables the practice of Buddhist techniques of Vipassana meditation under qualified supervision.

School of Humanities and Social Sciences

The school gives students an understanding of contemporary social issues through the inter-relationship of science, technology and management. It hosts an education programme that focuses on creating an understanding of the complexities of life in social context with emphasis on Indian cultural values and Buddhist ethics. The school is supported by a library with a large collection of books, journals, audio-visual aids and other learning materials.

School of Vocational Studies and Applied Sciences

The school of Vocational Studies & Applied Sciences offers courses in Applied Chemistry, Applied Physics, Applied Mathematics, Environmental Scinces and Food Processing & Technology.It offers integrated dual degree in Btech+Mtech/MBA in Food Processing & Technology.It is coming up as one of the premier institutions in applied sciences in north India.

Placements

Students that have been placed in various organisations are HCL, Honda, NIIT, Aditya Birla Group, TCS, Kirloskar, CRISIL, BlackRock, berkshire, Larsen & Toubro, Infosys, encyclopaedia britannica, Godrej Infotech, Matrix[disambiguation needed], AON, Tech Mahindra, Genpact, Elsevier, Huawei, Nestle and many more.

Gautam Buddha University

The university was established by the Uttar Pradesh Gautam Buddha University Act 2002 UP Act no. 9 of 2002 and began its first academic session in 2008. The university is fully funded by the NOIDA and GNIDA and recognized by the University Grants Commission. It is member of the Association of Indian Universities.
